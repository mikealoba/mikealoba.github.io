My General Physics 2 E-Portfolio

I. Brief Description

My General Physics 2 E-Portfolio is a collection of ALL your learning outputs in General Physics 2. It contains your works and achievements related to your adventures, reflections, quizzes and other artifacts of learning which are indicators of the attainment of all the intended learning outcomes for General Physics 2.

Using an application of your choice, it contains a highly organized assemblage of prints, pictures, diagrams, artworks, worked examples and quizzes that will all contribute to show how much you have achieved.

II. Specific Objectives

  1. show all your required and optional accomplishments in General Physics 2, Activities, Formative Assessments, both individual and group work.
  2. present evidences of reflective thinking and metacognition in your journey to different topics of General Physics 2.
  3. integrate your personality and your career goal to become the best that you can with your learning experiences in the course, and
  4. exhibit Communication, Creativity, Critical Thinking and Collaboration (in your group works) - the four Cs of 21st century learners

III. Materials Needed

  • copies of all your outputs in your Group Activities, Quizzes, Assignments and other Formative Assessments
  • a compilation of your My General Physics 2 Reflective Journal Submissions
  • diagrams, pictures complementary to the topic/content
  • pictures and/or photos (Short video clips are optional.)

IV. Components of the Portfolio

  1. Title Page
    • contains the Title (personalized and creative) of your very own portfolio
  2. Activity Outputs
    • copies or scanned photos; print and pictures or diagrams associated with the content
  3. My General Physics 2 E-Portfolio Reflective E-Journal
    • for all your weekly reflections and realizations
    • musings and muni-muni moments
    • related to your learning outcomes and experiences in the course.
  4. Conclusion: General Physics 2 Reflective Essay with your own creative title
    • aims to explain in your own words, in 2 pages only, ten(10) most valuable insights that you had gained from the course and that you believe will be a wellspring of inspiration for you in your future career.
    • may include pictures and artwork
    • format - Times New Roman, size 12, double space, 1 inch-margin (top, bottom, left, and right)
